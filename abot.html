OUR PHILOSOPHY

We obesess so you never have to.

Specter was founded in 2018 with a single conviction: that the phones people carry every day should be extraordinary. Not just fast — beautiful. Not just functional — genuinely delightful.

We spend years on a single design. We test cameras on five continents. We hire material scientists to find alloys that feel perfect in your hand. We argue about the exact weight of a notification sound.

Every decision is made with one question: does this make someone’s life better? If the answer is anything other than a clear yes, we go back to the drawing board.
